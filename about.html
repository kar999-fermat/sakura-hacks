Who are we?

Sakura Hacks is an organization of high schoolers with a love for computer science and technology. We are dedicated to sharing our passion with younger students by providing opportunities for learning, fun, and competition. We selected the name Sakura Hacks because cherry blossoms traditionally represent renewal, community growth, and fresh beginnings. Our mission is to establish a welcoming entry environment for middle schoolers.

Locations

Sakura Hacks is based in Naperville, Illinois.
